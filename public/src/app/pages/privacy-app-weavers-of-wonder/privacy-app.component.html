Weavers of Wonder App data protection notice

Deborah Sutton (Lancaster University) does not take or retain any of your personal data from app access. Basic network information, such as IP addresses, may be collected when the app communicates with our internal systems. Some additional data may be collected by third-party tools we use. Please see information below. Lancaster University are not responsible for data collection by third party tools we use.

If you grant access, The Weavers of Wonder app will collect location data - however we personally do not transfer this data from your device. All processing for sending push notifications based on location data happens locally on your device.

Google Maps

We use Google Maps SDK to display the locations on our app. Google may collect your mobile device’s location, as well as your IP address and network information. See the full Google data privacy policy here.

Firebase Crashlytics

You can choose to opt in to ‘Crashlytics’ collection. This will allow us to receive information via Google’s ‘Firebase’ platform, about how well the app is running on your device. We will receive logs and error reports.

This data is retained for 90 days. We can’t identify you from this data. See the full Firebase privacy policy here.

Firebase Dynamic Links

‘Dynamic Links‘ is another tool we use via Google’s ‘Firebase’ platform. ‘Dynamic links’ uses technical information from your device to open to a specific page or context. This means, for example, when scanning a QR code we can take you directly to specific content within our app.

Dynamic Links only stores device specs and IP addresses temporarily, to provide the service. We can’t identify you from this data. See the full Firebase privacy policy here.

Lancaster University is the designated project data processor and controller. Deborah Sutton is the nominated contact; email her on d.sutton@lancaster.ac.uk.
